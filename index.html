LOOKING GLASS

live camera streaming and hosting

A User-Controlled streaming site,
Made for Everyone

Looking Glass is a website that aims to provide limitless access to live hosting and streaming to everyone - from security companies to students, warzone reporters to event presenters etc.

//

Host or Watch user-uploaded
Camera Streams

Explore the world through user-uploaded livestreams of camera feeds on our interactive map; be it a CCTV or Phone camera. Need to check the traffic? Want host a stream for an event? Dying to watch live hurricane footage? Or maybe checking out a dodgy neighborhood? Looking Glass has you covered.

//

In-depth Statistics for
Analysis and Interpretation

Another major feature is our Statistic recording, where we gauge all sorts of variables and numbers to provide stats such as cam ratings, most popular cam type, cam view 'heatmaps' and much more.

//

How it all works

Google Maps Plugin

Looking Glass currently integrates Google Maps for its key interactive map feature using Javascript, with built-in markers as the selectable livestream icons. Future updates will use a separate and more customizable map API. The site also uses Material Design Lite as the key framework.

//

Databases and
Dedicated Servers (COMING SOON)

Using a massive database centered in PHP and MySQL; all the profiles, stats and other components are safely stored to be easily shown for your public viewing. Currently we're using Firebase to provide a dynamic geolocation system! We also use our own dedicated servers to host hundreds of cam streams from around the world, making them directly watchable by you!

//

Privacy and Terms:
The Big Brother effect

We're dedicated to keeping all streams PG and constantly have admins on to check streams and user reports. Any provocative or inappropriate streams will be removed and the user banned.
In that respect, yes - all streams are monitored and information and stats are gathered based on these streams. That said, none of your private data will ever be shared or sold. We guarantee privacy.
